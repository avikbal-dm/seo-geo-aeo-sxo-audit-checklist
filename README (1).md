# SEO, GEO, AEO & SXO Audit Checklist

**A complete SEO audit checklist and workflow for the AI search era. Technical SEO, on-page, GEO, answer engine optimization, AI Overviews, and search experience, in one tick-as-you-go workbook.**

> Most SEO checklists are stuck in 2020. They cover title tags and backlinks and say nothing about whether ChatGPT cites you, whether you appear in Google's AI Overviews, or whether Perplexity can read your page. This one covers all of it: classic SEO plus the AI search systems that now decide visibility.

---

## Why this checklist is different

Search stopped being ten blue links.

A growing share of queries now end inside an AI answer: Google's AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini. Your page can be technically perfect and still be invisible to all of them, because the rules for getting *cited by an AI engine* are not the same as the rules for ranking in classic search.

Most audit checklists never caught up. They optimize for a search experience that is shrinking and ignore the one that is growing.

This workbook covers both. It runs from the technical foundations every site needs, through on-page optimization, and into the four systems that decide AI-era visibility:

- **GEO** (Generative Engine Optimization) so AI engines can read and cite you
- **AEO** (Answer Engine Optimization) so you appear in the answer, not just the links
- **AIO** (AI Overviews) so you are eligible for Google's AI answer citations
- **SXO** (Search Experience Optimization) so the experience matches the searcher's intent

It is built as a tick-as-you-go system. Open a tab, work the list, check the boxes, move on.

## How to use it

1. Start on the **Start Here** tab to orient yourself.
2. Pick the tab that matches your task. Launching a site? Use New Website Launch. Publishing a page? Use New Page Launch. Running a full technical sweep? Use Technical SEO.
3. Work the checklist top to bottom. Tick each item as you complete it.
4. For AI-era visibility, run the **GEO**, **AEO**, **AIO**, and **SXO** tabs on every important page, not just the technical ones.
5. Use the **Weekly** and **Monthly** workflow tabs to keep the work recurring, not one-off.
6. Keep the **Priority Tracker** open to see status across every sheet.

## Frequently asked questions

**What is the difference between SEO, GEO, AEO, and AIO?**
SEO optimizes for classic search rankings. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) optimizes so AI engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity can read and cite your content. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures content to win the direct answer. AIO targets Google's AI Overviews specifically. The [concepts guide](docs/concepts-and-how-to-use.md) explains each in depth.

**Do I need paid tools to use this?**
No. The checklist works on its own, and the core checks can be done with free tools: Google Search Console, PageSpeed Insights, and a free crawler. Paid tools speed things up but are optional.

**How often should I run the audit?**
It depends on the tab. New Page and On-Page checks run every time you publish or edit. Technical SEO and Content Audit run monthly or quarterly. The Weekly and Monthly Workflow tabs set the recurring cadence.

**Will this help me show up in ChatGPT and AI Overviews?**
That is exactly what the GEO, AEO, and AIO tabs are for. They cover the structural and content signals that make a page readable and citable by AI engines. See the [how search works guide](docs/how-search-works-now.md).

**Can I use this for client work?**
Yes. The license allows commercial use. Many consultants run a version of this per client.
